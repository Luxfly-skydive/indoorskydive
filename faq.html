Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about birthday parties and indoor skydiving at LUXFLY.

What ages are eligible for indoor skydiving at LUXFLY?+

Children must be at least 4 years old. We offer two separate birthday packages: Children (ages 4–13) and Teens (ages 14–17), each with age-appropriate pricing and flight experiences.

How many flights are included per participant?+

Every participant receives 2 individual flights in the wind tunnel, each lasting approximately 60 seconds. A certified instructor is present inside the tunnel for every flight.

What is the minimum group size for a birthday booking?+

All birthday packages require a minimum of 6 participants. This applies to both weekday and weekend bookings across all age groups.

What equipment do participants need to bring?+

Nothing — we provide all flight equipment including a professional flight suit, helmet, goggles, and earplugs. Participants should wear comfortable clothing and closed-toe lace-up shoes.

How long should we plan to be at LUXFLY?+

The full birthday experience takes approximately 1.5 to 2 hours. This includes check-in, safety briefing, all flights, an instructor demonstration, and private party room time.

What safety measures are in place?+

All flights are one-on-one with a certified instructor inside the wind tunnel. Equipment is professionally maintained and inspected. A detailed safety briefing is given before every session. Parents can observe all flights from the viewing gallery.

What is the HighFly upgrade?+

HighFly is our premium add-on (€15 per person) that increases wind speed and altitude for a more advanced flight experience. It is strongly recommended for teens and is available for all age groups.

Can parents and non-flying guests attend?+

Absolutely. Parents and guests have full access to the viewing gallery beside the tunnel and are welcome to join the group in the private birthday room.

What food and drink is included?+

Every booking includes a private birthday room with tableware, unlimited still and flavoured water, and a music system. You are welcome to bring your own birthday cake, snacks, and decorations.

What are the pricing options?+

Kids (4–13): €32/child weekday, €69.16/child weekend (first 6), then €59. Teens (14–17): €45/teen weekday, €79/teen weekend (first 6), then €59. HighFly upgrade: +€15/person.

How do I make a booking?+

Contact us directly via email at hello@luxfly.eu or WhatsApp at +32 2 320 55 09. Our team will help you select a date and package.

What is the cancellation and rescheduling policy?+

For cancellations or date changes, please contact support@luxfly.eu as early as possible. We will do our best to accommodate schedule changes subject to availability.

Where is LUXFLY located and how do I get there?+

LUXFLY Indoor Skydiving is at Rue de Grass 103, 6700 Sterpenich, Belgium — close to the Luxembourg border. Free parking is available on site.
