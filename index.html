Good to Know

Quick answers before you book.

Ages & Weight

Open to children from age 4. Maximum 120 kg per flyer. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult on site.

Health & Safety

Not suitable for anyone with previous shoulder dislocation, heart conditions, or pregnancy. All other guests welcome.

What to Wear

Lace-up trainers, fitted clothing (no skirts or loose tops). Long hair tied back. We provide flight suits, helmets, goggles and earplugs.

Cancellation

Free changes up to 48 hours before your slot. Late cancellations are non-refundable.
